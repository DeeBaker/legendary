Allegro Families –

Our showcases are only less than 2 weeks away and we need to make sure you are informed! Believe it or not, some people don’t read their e-mails/newsletters and still are not aware of costume & showcase info.

Please READ this e-mail – Staying informed is the best way to alleviate stress in this process. We want you to enjoy your showcase experiences and to NOT have stress!! *Remember – all showcase times, costumes info and location is on our website.

Happening This Week!

Picture Day & Student Appreciation

June 4th at Allegro!

Don’t forget to wear your costume along with any accessories like tights, socks and shoes as specified for your costume. Please have your hair done as directed and arrive at least 30 minutes early to allow time for parking, payment and lining up. Light makeup is optional.

Order Forms are available at Allegro and there is one order form in your family folder so that you can plan on which packages you would like to order. Payment is to be made to Nigel Cooper Photography.

*Even if you do not want to purchase pictures, please come and be in the picture so that you can be in the group picture and be on our wall next year: )

Please keep in mind that parking will be tight, we also have overflow parking at the Health Point Resource Center.
